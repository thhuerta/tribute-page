Black Frost

The coolest and most broken persona.

Black Frost, a character from the SMT and Persona videogame franchise.

here is a list of reasons why

  • he's cute af
  • initially he has a light weakness, but if you level him up he can get rid of it by resisting light skills from enemies
  • he absorbs ice magic from enemies, healing himself in the process
  • you can fuse him pretty early if you know how
  • he reflects dark skills
  • he blocks fire skills
  • he deals medium dark damage, and heavy fire and ice damage
  • he is a persona from the fool arcana, so you should be able to fuse him with a higher level than other personas

if you want to get to know him better, you can do it by visitting this character Wiki or buying an SMT, Persona or Soul Hackers game.
